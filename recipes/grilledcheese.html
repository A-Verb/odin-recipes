Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Sliced Cheese
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Butter the bread and place the bread butter-side down on a hot skillet or frying pan.
  2. Top with cheese, then place another slice of bread on top.
  3. Continue cooking until the cheese has melted and the bread has browned.
